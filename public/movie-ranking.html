Best Anime/Mangas so far By Revy

I. Animes

Shin Seiki Evangelion

Dark fantasy, sci-fi anime. If your life is full of hapiness, give it a try. It's gonna make you cry.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Best shounen in recent years in my opinion, probably best action anime so far by Mappa Studio.
New contents, exciting verse with rich stories and diverse characters.
Don't miss it, guys!

High school DxD

I didn't intend to find out about this anime but...
I know... 18+ content, spicy stuffs, but it is fun, wild story with goofy characters.
Give it a try, men of culture!

II. Mangas

Berserk

Needless to say, This one is a masterpiece. If you think Evangelion is dark, Berserk is the definition
of depression. Read this if you want to become a man, boys. Badass protagonist, fast-pace storyline, full
of emotional moments. God-tier manga!
Ps: Author die, so... we won't be able to see Guts finding his own happiness anymore...

Summer Time Rendering

When it comes to time traveling and scifi/horrow mangas, this one by Yasuki Tanaka is top-knot.
Thrilling, scary moments with unexpected twists will make your brain overflow with informations.
Try to keep up with protagonist in his "one in a life time aventure".

DeathNote

Common, man. Who don't know this manga? Among all of the manga I have read, this one is unique. What will you do if you have the power to control people's lives?
Will you be able to keep your sanity knowing you kill someone without even have to touch them.
NO ones will be able to find out about your identity, isn't it?
Ps: there is no antagonists or protagonists in this manga... You are the one to decide so... Enjoy!
